Dicodon optimizer

Introduction

This is a in-browser app to optimze gene sequences on the dicodon level for enhanced gene expression.

You need

  • a coding sequence (either DNA-codons or an amino acid sequence)
  • a frequency table of dicodon usage (which you cane derive from a coding sequence fasta below)

Then follow the forms below until you receive your output.
